# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- being respectful and constructive
- focusing feedback on the code and the idea
- being open to different viewpoints

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment or abusive language
- trolling or inflammatory comments
- sharing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this code of conduct.
